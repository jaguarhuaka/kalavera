Privacy Policy

ContractAI · Last updated: May 25, 2026

TL;DR — We don't want your data. We don't collect it. We don't have it.

What we collect

  • On this site: nothing. No accounts, no tracking pixels, no analytics, no cookies beyond what Cloudflare uses for basic anti-abuse.
  • Payments: handled entirely by Stripe (cards) or your own Monero wallet (XMR). We never see your card number, your billing address, or your wallet keys. See Stripe's policy at stripe.com/privacy

What we never do

No Google, no Facebook pixel, no Meta, no third-party analytics. No newsletters. No selling or sharing data — there is nothing to sell.
